osunator / human or machine?

blinded replay study

What this is

You'll watch 20 pairs of osu! replay clips (~20 seconds each). In every pair, one clip is a real human play and the other was generated by a neural network same map, same section. Your job: pick the human.

Takes about 15 minutes. You can change any answer on the review screen before submitting. After you submit, you'll see your score and which clips were which.

What we store: your answers, an experience bracket you choose, rough timing, and a hashed (non-reversible) network identifier to detect duplicate submissions. No account, no name, no email.

Results feed an academic writeup on whether generated replays are distinguishable from human play. Please don't participate twice.

One question first

How much osu! have you played? (affects analysis only, there are no wrong answers)

which clip is the human? Watch both, then choose.

Clips loop, so take your time.

Review your answers

Click any row (or any cell in the strip above) to rewatch and change your pick. Submitting is final.
